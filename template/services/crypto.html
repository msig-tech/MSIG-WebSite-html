What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital payment system that does not rely on banks to verify transactions. It is a peer-to-peer (P2P) sharing system that allows anyone to send and receive payments anywhere. When you transfer funds in cryptocurrencies, the transactions are recorded in a public ledger. Cryptocurrencies are stored in digital wallets.
