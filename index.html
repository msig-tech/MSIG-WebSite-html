Diversification to Reduce Risk

Forex

Forex, also known as the foreign exchange market, FX, or currency trading, is an international decentralized market where all global currencies are traded. The forex market is the largest liquid market in the world with an average daily trading volume exceeding $5 trillion.

Crypto

A cryptocurrency is a digital payment system that doesn't rely on banks to verify transactions. It operates on a peer-to-peer (P2P) sharing system, allowing everyone to send and receive payments anywhere. When you transfer funds in cryptocurrencies, the transactions are recorded in a public ledger. Cryptocurrencies are stored in digital wallets.

Real estate

Property management is the management applied to buildings, whether residential, commercial, or industrial, as well as to land.
This also involves the management of an external real estate asset, including rent management services, tenant search, administrative document processing, maintenance, and conflict management.
The expertise of the professional saves time for the owner; indeed, the real estate market requires knowledge of the local market, legal or administrative matters that the owner may not necessarily possess.
